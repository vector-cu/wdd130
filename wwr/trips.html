Beginner’s River Run

Perfect for first-timers and families, this scenic float features Class I–II rapids with plenty of time to relax and take in the views. Our guides make sure you feel safe and confident every step of the way.

Intermediate Rapids Challenge

Experience the excitement of Class III–IV rapids on this half-day adventure. Great for those who have rafted before and want more thrills without committing to a full day.

Extreme Rapids Expedition

For adrenaline seekers only! Tackle roaring Class IV–V rapids with our most experienced guides. Includes safety briefings, gear, and lunch along the way.

Scenic Multi-Day Adventure

Immerse yourself in nature on this 3-day journey through breathtaking canyons and pristine wilderness. Camp under the stars, enjoy hearty meals by the river, and conquer Class II–III rapids along the way.
